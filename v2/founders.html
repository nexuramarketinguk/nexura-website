Why we built it

Good venues shouldn't have to choose between
running the place and marketing it.

London has hundreds of brilliant independent pubs, restaurants and cafés staffed by people who are far too busy to spend Sunday night writing an Instagram caption.

Agencies are too expensive and too slow. In-house hires are too narrow. DIY tools add another thing to log into. So we built Nexura — an AI-native marketing agency that works through the one app venue owners already have open.
