Eight disciplines.

Every plan includes every service. These are the eight things Nexura runs, end-to-end, for every venue on our books.

Social Media

Instagram, TikTok and Facebook — written, designed, shot and scheduled. We treat every grid as editorial, not a template library.
  • Monthly content calendar, planned around your rota and specials
  • Feed posts, reels, stories and TikToks — all on-brand
  • Captions, hashtags and reply management
  • Quarterly on-site photography (House & Group plans)
Daily cadence

Google Business

Most venues ignore this. It's the single biggest lever for getting found on a Friday night. We run it every week.
  • Opening hours, menus, holiday overrides, service areas
  • Weekly posts, new photos, Q&A moderation
  • Review responses synced with your tone
  • Local SEO schema and categorisation
Weekly updates

Reputation

Every review gets a reply within 24 hours. One-stars handled with care — not templated, not defensive. Five-stars amplified across your channels.
  • Google, TripAdvisor, Resy, OpenTable, Facebook
  • Tone-matched replies that sound like you wrote them
  • Escalation paths for anything that needs a human eye
  • Monthly sentiment trend reporting
< 24h response

Local SEO

The work that makes your venue the answer to “best roast near me.” Unflashy, ongoing, and it compounds over months.
  • Keyword research specific to your area + food / drink type
  • On-page schema, menu markup, events markup
  • Citation + directory submissions
  • Competitor keyword monitoring
Monthly optimisation

Email & SMS

Regulars come back when reminded. We write, design and send every campaign — monthly newsletters, seasonal pushes, event nudges.
  • Mailing list growth + list hygiene
  • Seasonal campaigns (bank holidays, Valentine's, summer terrace)
  • Event + booking reminders via SMS
  • Automation flows — welcome series, win-back, post-visit
1–2 sends / month

Content & Copy

Menus, specials, landing copy, blog posts, press bios — anything written, in your voice, not a travel-magazine pastiche.
  • Menu copy, seasonal specials, tasting notes

  • Press releases, launch announcements
On request

Continuous
