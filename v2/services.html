Eight things we run for every venue. In one thread.

Every plan gets every service. There's nothing to choose, nothing to upgrade — these are the eight things Nexura does, end to end, for every venue on our books.

01 / 08

Instagram, TikTok & Facebook

Posts, reels, stories — written, designed and scheduled around your service rhythm. Every grid is treated as editorial; nothing comes out of a template library.
  • Monthly content calendar planned around your rota + specials
  • Feed posts, reels, stories, TikToks — all on-brand
  • Captions, hashtags, DM replies
  • Quarterly on-site photography (House & Group plans)
Daily
02 / 08

Google Business Profile

Most venues treat Google as set-and-forget. It's the single biggest lever for getting found on a Friday night — we run it every week.
  • Opening hours, menus, holiday overrides, service areas
  • Weekly posts, new photos, Q&A moderation
  • Review responses synced with your tone of voice
  • Local SEO schema + categorisation
Weekly
03 / 08

Review replies

Every review, every platform, replied to in your voice within 24 hours. One-stars get care — not defensive templates. Five-stars get amplified where they sell.
  • Google, TripAdvisor, Resy, OpenTable, Facebook
  • Tone-matched replies that sound like you wrote them
  • Escalation for anything that needs a human eye
  • Monthly sentiment trend reporting
< 24h

Local SEO

The work that makes your venue the answer to “best roast near me” — unflashy, ongoing, and it compounds over months.
  • Keyword research specific to your area + cuisine
  • On-page schema, menu markup, events markup
  • Citation + directory submissions
  • Competitor keyword monitoring
Monthly
05 / 08

Email & SMS

Regulars come back when reminded. We write, design and send every campaign — monthly newsletters, seasonal pushes, event reminders.
  • Mailing list growth + hygiene
  • Seasonal campaigns (bank holidays, Valentine's, summer)
  • Event + booking reminders via SMS
  • Automation flows — welcome, win-back, post-visit
1–2 / month

Menu & web copy

Menus, specials, landing copy, blog posts, press bios — anything written, in your voice, not a travel-magazine pastiche.

  • Press releases + launch announcements
On request

Competitor watch

What the venue two streets over is posting, pricing and scoring at. Quietly tracked. Flagged to you when it matters — not in a weekly noise report.

Continuous

Monthly reports

One page a month. What grew. What flopped. What we're changing. No vanity metrics, no 40-tab PDFs. A read you'll actually read.
