Why we built it

Agencies are too slow.
In-house hires are
too narrow.

The UK has hundreds of brilliant independent pubs, restaurants and cafés staffed by people far too busy to spend Sunday nights writing Instagram captions — let alone managing Google listings, local SEO and email.

Traditional agencies are expensive and slow. In-house marketers are narrow and hard to hire. DIY tools just add another thing to log into. So we built Nexura — an AI-native marketing agency that works through the one app venue owners already have open.
